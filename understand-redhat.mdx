## What is Red Hat OpenShift and what does it do? <br/>

The customer has a choice when it comes to the target infrastructure. It could be on-premises or on the public cloud. It could be virtual machine based or containerized. Whenever it applies, you should make every effort to land the workload on the Red Hat OpenShift Container Platform to ensure we "win the platform battle". Talk about Cloud Native and the benefit it brings to the customers IT operations. Position Red Hat OpenShift as the leading Kubernetes platform and how it accelerates hybrid cloud adoption.

When a customer chooses OCP, you, as a trusted advisor, must advise them on the proper sizing, storage solutions, patterns for reliability, service mesh, operations, DevOps / GitOps solutions, and so much more.
