## Responsibilities of Customer Success

In your <a href='https://w3.ibm.com/w3publisher/customersuccess/the-practice' target='_blank' rel='noreferrer noopener'>role as an IBM CSM</a>, you are the technical expert who works with our customers post sales to ensure their business goals are met with our software and services. CSMs deeply understand their customer's business via regular face-to-face and virtual contact. A CSM co-defines the roadmap, conducts workshops, presents demos, facilitates EBRs, and 
builds customer advocates at all levels of the account.

## CS coverage model

All client segments, <a href='https://w3.ibm.com/w3publisher/ibmsaleszone/gtm-guide-2024/clients-and-partners/client-segmentation'>Signature, Strategic, Select,</a> are eligible for CSM coverage.

* **Confirm value** Ensure successful deployment, adoption, and renewal of IBM technology.
* **Expand** Discover expansion opportunities within and across the product or service the client is using. Connect to renewal and sales teams and run retention plans when needed.

* Assists in Day 2 operational planning and execution

* Executes customer success plan to drive adoption post-deployment
* Engages customers both remotely and on-site
* Activates early conversations with customer and sales teams to renew and expand contracts
* Builds and executes retention and expansion plays

### CS Brand Architect

**CS Brand Architects** are technical experts who work with our customers post-sales and ensure customers' business goals are met with our software and services. CSM architects drive the deployment of prioritized <a href="https://w3.ibm.com/w3publisher/customersuccess/the-practice/customer-coverage-model" target='_blank' rel='noreferrer noopener'>CSM covered offerings</a> that accelerate modernization, Red Hat OpenShift, key acquisition products, and advance IBM’s Product Led Growth (PLG) initiatives.

IBM CSMs stay with the customer through the life of the contract to grow the account. They look for expansion opportunities within and across the product or service the customer is using and bring those insights to the broader account team. CSMs also partner with renewals and sellers to ensure the customer has a seamless renewal process.

### CS Account Specialist

**CS Account Specialists** are assigned to clients in the Diamond program and cover all software in reporting sections of Diamond ELAs.
As a <a href='https://w3.ibm.com/w3publisher/client-success-and-software-adoption/roles-responsibilities' target='_blank' rel='noreferrer noopener'>CS Account Specialist</a>, you are responsible for understanding the portfolio and the overall deployment strategy for <Link to='/onboard/ELA' target='_blank' rel='noreferrer noopener'>Diamond clients</Link>. CS Account Specialists work closely with clients, partners, and the client team to secure projects and sponsors, build deployment plans, guide clients into production, and ensure clients have the best experience with IBM technology.

### Digital CSM

**Digital CSMs** use data and automation to engage customers at scale in a way that makes each customer feel seen and heard without the need for direct and constant one-on-one contact.  They use a communication strategy built on product usage to progress customers through the user journey to realize value.  

<div className="expressive-content-list-group">
  <ExpressiveList
    title="Digital CSM responsibilities"
    titleType="expressive-02"
    background="true">

* **Onboard** Welcome the customer, ensure onboarding is completed, host one-to-many getting started webinars, and share self-service resources.
* **Deploy** Share deployment checklists.
* **Adopt** Share feature adoption guides.
* **Confirm value** Automated in-app and email ‘x’ days before renewal; email triggers to renewal and sales teams.
* **Expand** Data-driven expansion nurture.

</ExpressiveList>

### Digital CS Program Specialist

The **Digital CS Program Specialist** defines, builds, and maintains the digital nurture, automation, practices, and scalable content experiences necessary to ensure adoption and renewal for clients of PLG products.
This specialist defines the digital lifecycle phases and usage events that signal value and risk, identifies product instrumentation requirements, and builds digital nurture and automated triggers. 

<div className="expressive-content-list-group">
  <ExpressiveList
    title="Digital CS Program Specialist Responsibilities"
    titleType="expressive-02"
    background="true">

* For assigned products, work with Product Management to define and maintain the user journey and adoption level metrics which improve retention.
* Define instrumentation requirements to support digital understanding of user behavior and automation of both tech-touch and low-touch client engagement.
* Define and maintain functional workflows in Gainsight for use by the CS team.
* Write content and set up Journey Orchestrator programs in Gainsight.
* Define Customer Success practices and tooling necessary to support low-touch and tech-touch approach for PLG products.
* Work with the CAO team to identify and implement AI-based approaches to improve automation and business outcomes.
* Work with the CIO’s office and Gainsight team to implement data flows, system integration and reporting to facilitate Customer Success at scale. 


</ExpressiveList>
</div>

## A day in the life of a CSM

The following are best practices that the Customer Success Managers developed to help highlight what they do to perform at their best within their role.

<Accordion>
<AccordionItem title='Daily'>

**Tasks**

* Respond to new email or communications from customers or the account team. Route to the proper owner or schedule personal time to complete. (e.g., Support, Billing, Renewals, etc.)
* Review <Link to='/onboard/gainsight-cockpit'>Gainsight cockpit</Link> for open CTA's and upcoming due dates. Develop action plans as needed to close.
* Check progress against your goals for the week. Make sure each goal has actions and owners so the next steps are clear
* Communicate key takeaways by updating <Link to='/onboard/gainsight-timeline'>Gainsight timeline</Link> following customer meetings.
* Check in on Slack team channels, CoPs, #cs-at-ibm, #cs-ww-leadership

</AccordionItem>
<AccordionItem title='Weekly'>

**Tasks**

* Document meetings including progress, updates, or key outstanding items, and that they are documented in Gainsight using <Link to='/onboard/gainsight-timeline'>Gainsight timeline</Link>. 
* Interlock with Sales and Business Partners reviewing consumption, retention, defects and renewal
* Reflect on your customers' product experience. Identify gaps and share insights with the Community of Practice leaders and Offering Management using the prescribed feedback process (typically via Aha!).  
* Keep up with what's new with the growth offerings. Share with your customers!
* Set aside time to:
  * Progress your skill development plan
  * Read up on your customer's business and industry
  * Engage in the <a href='https://w3.ibm.com/w3publisher/customersuccess/community-connections/community-connections' target='_blank' rel='noreferrer noopener'>Communities of Practice.</a>  
  * Share feedback with others including your leadership team on what's working, and what could be even better.
* <a href='https://ibmrr.performnet.com/ibmrr/homePage.do' target='_blank' rel='noreferrer noopener'>Appreciate</a> those who've made a difference for you! On #ThankfulThursday share in #cs-at-ibm or send an IBMer Appreciation card.

</AccordionItem>
<AccordionItem title='Monthly'>

**Tasks**

* Lead meetings with customers, including use case priority review changes or roadblocks toward strategic objectives
* Be aware of key events for your customers (e.g., major announcements, production go-lives, etc.) in the month ahead. Be proactive about ensuring they have the help they need for things to go smoothly.  
* Update, closeout, or create new Success Plans in Gainsight. Consider conducting a retrospective to discover what worked well and what could have been even better.
* Evaluate CSM Sentiment & Health scores to ensure accuracy in tools like Gainsight, ISC and Cadet (as applicable)
* Review your Gainsight CSM dashboard for any new renewals that are coming up in the next 6 months.  If you have one, be sure to <Link to='/expand-renew/renew-offerings'>take action with the renewal team.</Link>
* Check out the IBM events calendar for any events that would be relevant to your customer. Invite and attend with them when appropriate. These are great opportunities to have meaningful discussions about their strategic objectives and how IBM technology can accelerate. Be aware of key events for your customer's industry as well.
* Participate in your Specialties Community of Practice meeting found <a href='https://w3.ibm.com/w3publisher/customersuccess/community-connections/community-connections' target='_blank' rel='noreferrer noopener'>HERE</a>
* Share a best practice with a colleague or the community<br/><br/>

**Personal Development**

* Set skill development goals for the month ahead
* Allocate and complete specific learning/enablement time toward your professional goals
* Update your Checkpoint Goal progress

</AccordionItem>
<AccordionItem title='Quarterly'>

**Tasks**

* Meet with the account team to evaluate opportunities and deployment planning for customer business outcomes/strategic objectives
* Conduct CS  Executive Business Review with customer stakeholders highlighting achievement of business goals, and upcoming deployment plans and align on new business goals
 

**Personal Development**

* Review your personal goals and ensure alignment 
* Send feedback request or complete feedback in Checkpoint



</AccordionItem>
<AccordionItem title='Semi-annual'>

**Personal Development**

* Plan your career conversation and schedule with your manager/upline
* Review and refresh your credentials and expertise in <a href='https://w3.ibm.com/careers/' target='_blank' rel='noreferrer noopener'>Your Career@IBM</a>
* Set your "Open to Explore" preferences in Your Career @IBM and possible Sales <a href='https://w3.ibm.com/w3publisher/advancing-your-success/careers-for-sellers/career-journeys' target='_blank' rel='noreferrer noopener'>career journeys</a>
* Consider participation in your local site, Business Resources Group (BRG) or other professional groups

</AccordionItem>
<AccordionItem title='Annual'>

**Leadership Tasks**

* Summarize team accomplishments and lessons learned
* Consider using a Retrospective for past performance and future team planning
 

**Personal Development**

* Summarize quarterly, semi-annual accomplishments into your Annual Checkpoint update

</AccordionItem>
</Accordion>
