## What can I do in ISC?

ISC is a CRM platform used by Sales, Sales Leadership and Business Partners.

CSMs can use ISC to understand the use case behind a purchase and some of the activity that led up to the deal, collaborate with sellers and tech sellers via Chatter, access renewal information, and open CSM-Qualified Leads and assign them to a seller.


CSM Leaders can use ISC to research closing deals to see where a CSM might be needed. They can also check for use cases on opportunities that will be deployed by CSMs and follow up with sales leaders about missing ones and view the expansion opportunities created by their CSM team.

### Important: Gainsight remains your primary tool.

ISC is a tool to help you learn about your clients. You are not expected to manage your work in ISC, own and progress deals, or extract and compare data from ISC to Gainsight.  Remember that the ISC ACV will rarely match the ACV shown in Gainsight since Gainsight only shows ACV at risk of renewal.

<AccordionItem title='What is a Deployment Opportunity in ISC?'>

**What is a Deployment Opportunity in ISC?**

Much like a CSM's deployment growth plan, a Deployment Opportunity is how a tech seller tracks the deployment of a use case in ISC. When you are researching your customer, you might want to see the Deployment Opportunities recorded by the tech seller and reach out to understand more about them.
</AccordionItem>

</AccordionItem>

<AccordionItem title='Am I expected to work in Sales Cloud now?'>

**Am I expected to work in Sales Cloud now?**

No. As a CSM, your primary tool remains Gainsight.  You've been granted access to ISC to help you research customers and enable you to find and interact with the sales teams for your customers. You can also create Expansion Opportunities and hand them to sellers to drive to closure.
