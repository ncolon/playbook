When your customer has a use case that requires them to move from a point product to an IBM Cloud Pak, you should use the Cloud Pak modernization guide. It’s important to note that use case analysis is extremely important and allows the IBM team to decompose the high-level requirements to a more detailed list of real requirements needed to achieve the client’s business objective. 

## Cloud Pak modernization guide

The <a href='https://pages.github.ibm.com/csm-playbook/playbook/asset-modernization/' target='_blank' rel='noreferrer noopener'>Cloud Pak modernization guide</a> contains initial modernization guidance for each Cloud Pak. Be sure to expand each section to see all the pertinent guidance.

If you need help in building a customized recommendation for your customer, please reach out to the <a href='https://w3.ibm.com/w3publisher/customersuccess/community-connections/community-connections' target='_blank' rel='noreferrer noopener'>practice leads, who are listed in the community practices pages on w3.</a>
