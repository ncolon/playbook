## Pricing Objectives

Simple – Purchasing Supply Chain Suite via single part  – SCIS AppPoints  .

Understandable – Pricing terms are easy for customers to understand, predict, monitor and measure as well as for sellers to quote (number of AppPoints needed)

Extensible - Land and expand for customers.  CT to TS to Advance AI, to Order Promising

Measurable – Ability to easily monitor and measure to ensure tacking against any overages

Flexible - Simple consumption model for customers, ability to utilize and apply  AppPoints for whatever their needs are.

Consistent - Keep key metrics as consistent as possible across the Supply Chain Intelligence suite

Expandable - Customers have ways to purchase expansion beyond guard rails in the products to avoid being hit with overage charges.

Incentives – Customers who purchase more AppPoints are provided extra capacity for their investment, which is a huge benfit to the customers
