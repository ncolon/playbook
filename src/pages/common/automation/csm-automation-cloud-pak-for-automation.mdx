## What is IBM Cloud Pak for Business Automation and what does it do? <br/>
**IBM Cloud Pak for Business Automation** is a modular set of integrated software components, built for any hybrid cloud, designed to automate work and accelerate business growth. This end-to-end automation platform helps you analyze workflows, design AI-infused apps with low-code tooling, assign tasks to bots and track performance.

Learn more about the <a href="https://ibm-cloud-architecture.github.io/refarch-dba/use-cases/accounts-pay" target='_blank' rel='noreferrer noopener'>use cases for IBM Cloud Pak for Business Automation</a> including:

* Accounts payable
* HR onboarding applications
* ODM-WML integration
* Refund request
* Onboarding Automation
* Shared services

## What are the pain points that IBM Cloud Pak for Business Automation covers?

The key pain points that IBM Cloud Pak for Business Automation addresses are related to starting and scaling digital initiatives to meet increased business performance. <br/>

**Business data is inaccessible and siloed**  Business data is either hidden in manual documents, multiple systems, or not in the correct formats for easy actionability. For example, product usage data for clients that sit in multiple systems can’t be easily pulled into one view – making it difficult to effectively cross-sell opportunities at the right point in time. Or, a new regulation comes that requires a different approach to managing personal identification information (PII), Clients are not able to track down all the places that PII lives in their systems – putting their business at risk for hefty fines, or worse. <br/><br/>
**Performance gaps caused by manual systems prevent efficiency**  For example, customer service reps who have to manually enter data into multiple systems to approve applications experience delays to customers and are prone to errors. Without the ability to track key metrics across the company, it’s difficult to pinpoint where those companies should focus their attention first to have the greatest impact on operations as a whole. <br/><br/>
**Execution on key initiatives is slowed**  Execution on key initiatives, particularly the ability for line of business (LOBs) to quickly respond to changing demands, is slowed because they don’t have the right tools available that they can use to quickly make changes themselves. The LOB must rely on shared IT resources or outsourced resources with projects that take several cycles because of unclear requirements due to a lack of subject matter expertise (SME) involvement in the process. <br/><br/>


## The steps to deploy and who is responsible for what, when?

IBM Cloud Pak for Business Automation follows the traditional model of <br/><br/>
**Identify a pain point, sponsor and use case**<br/>
The IBM team of Sales, Tech Sales and potentially CSM can use the list of pain points above or a Client Engineering engagement to identify use cases and sponsors.<br/>
**Demonstrate how a given pain point can be addressed (demo)** Help your customers understand <a href="https://ibm.box.com/s/1rpp43vvck9kgykfw1nz0wib6zvigwq2" target='_blank' rel='noreferrer noopener'>the value of IBM Cloud Paks</a>.  This client-facing deck makes it clear what capabilities are unique to the pak and what is available standalone.<br/>
**Prove value proof of experience (PoX)**<br/>
Tech Sales, Client Engineering, IBM Cloud Pak for Business Automation TAM and CSM can work together to connect the product to the customers' systems and prove the capabilities to resolve the pain points.<br/>
**Implement a project**<br/>
Technology Expert Labs can take ownership of an implementation project using their <a href='https://ibm.seismic.com/Link/Content/DC9PHp4jCPc6G87MggFJFXBmXPm8' target='_blank' rel='noreferrer noopener'>pre-packaged offerings.</a> The TAM and CSM can help guide the customer through the implementation project.
