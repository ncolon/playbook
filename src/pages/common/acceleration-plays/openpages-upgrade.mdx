**Overall value proposition of IBM OpenPages:**

OpenPages with Watson gives your client the complete view of sensitive data and how it's being used throughout the organization, delivered through a simple award-winning UI that makes your client the risk expert that they need to be. From physical to metadata, OpenPages provides a complete view of how sensitive data is used, stored, and accessed throughout the organization with embedded AI, automation, and security.

The focus of this engagement model is to ensure enablement, support, and delivery of assets and resources to faclitate a client to upgrade their OpenPage Governance Risk and Compliance **(GRC)** deployment to OpenPages 9.0.

## Engage client

**Led by:** CSM, Software Seller or Services Seller

**Audience:** Executive/Sponsor

**What are the key value propositions to upgrading OpenPages?**

Take advantage of New Features - Application Enhancements, AI Integrations, and Platform improvements

**Note:** 8.2 is end of support September 30, 2023 - avoid extended support fees (on-premise) or lack of support

**Why Upgrade?**

* Evolving needs from governance programs in organizations

* Evolving complexity of the governance ecosystem 

* Inability to manage or report on AI

* Cost of adaptability

**New Features to be aware of in OpenPages 9.0:**

* Latest OS - Windows 2022 & RHEL 8

* CyberArk - on premise only

* Questionnaire enhancements for more automated actions

* Custom error messaging for workflows

* Automated workflow progression

* Expansion of filter logic

**Lastly, but most important is the integration of AI into GRC:**

Watson integrations help radically improve user decision making and accelerate customer time to value:

- Insight through trained AI models on IBM cloud, leveraging tags, across all objects and activities.
- Users can run models, set tags, or set fields manually or automatically.
- Administrators can set model confidence thresholds for display and customize the display of model notifications and style for the user.
- Custom Model Integration to support corporate initiatives like PII identification and common governance descriptive standards.

## OpenPages upgrade briefing

**Led by:** TEL Solution Architect

**Supporting Roles:** CSM, TEL Service Seller

The focus of the briefing is to share additional details on OpenPages 9.0 and upgrade approaches with a standard upgrade client briefing.
Introduce upgrade questionnaire, which is used to determine current implementation details (SaaS /on Premise,  % of customization etc). 

**Prepare:**

- CSM should review and use the [Standard briefing template](https://ibm.box.com/s/hu1d3smqknkyzo1y19mq8ujmuor5d83x) - customize to each client.
- Introduce the [Upgrade Questionnaire:](https://ibm.box.com/s/zd0wwbb1gqb59ftw1ds0i8tigadwwasr) 
- CSM and TEL Seller can reference these Slack Channels for additional information: [Link](https://ibm-industrysolutions.slack.com/archives/C6JPJ6S0H)

The **outcome** agreement to move foreward to an assessment. 

## Propose, Negotiate and Close phase


**Led by:** Technology Expert Labs Seller

The purpose of the Propose, Negotiate and Close is to close a services deal. The IBM Technology Expert Labs Sales team: Digital/Services Seller, Solution Architect/Solution Engineer will present the customer with a Services proposal for OpenPages 9.0 offerings.

There are no required inputs by the CSM; however, as a CSM, you should remain engaged with the client.

## Implement, Build, and Learn Services of Technology Expert Labs


**Led by:** Technology Expert Labs Delivery

During the delivery phase, the IBM Technology Expert Labs Delivery team will perform the services as agreed upon in the Propose Negotiate and Close Phase.

There are no required inputs by the CSM; however, as a CSM, you should remain engaged with the client.
