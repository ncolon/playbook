## Scope

**IBM Customer Care solutions** personalize the customer experience through analytics and automation to help organizations build strong customer relationships through any channel. IBM's Customer Care portfolio is positioned to leverage the latest advancements in artificial intelligence to help a brand or organization’s end-users complete task. It is made up of three product areas: watsonx Assistant, Watson Discovery and Watson Speech Services.

- watsonx Assistant: Deliver consistent and intelligent customer care across all channels and touchpoints with conversational AI. 

- Watson Discovery: Accelerate business decisions and processes by applying natural-language, AI-powered content analysis to discover answers and insights faster.

- Watson Speech to Text: Use AI technology to understand the customer voice and turn it into usable, searchable text in real time. 

- Watson Text to Speech: Convert written text into natural-sounding audio in a variety of languages. 

## Customer Care and generative AI
IBM watsonx Assistant offers customers the flexibility to use IBM proprietary fine-tuned models embedded out of the box in watsonx Assistant, to connect to open-source or IBM proprietary LLMs hosted on watsonx.ai, or to connect to LLMs from 3rd party providers.
