## Getting Started Activities

* **Account Assignments:** Confirm account assignments received; If not, notify your FLM ASAP
* **CADET Access:** Confirm CADET and Gainsight access. If don’t have access notify your Manager ASAP, so Charlie Carullo can grant access for Cadet or Brian Henry for Gainsight.
    * <a href="https://cadet.az14.dal.cpc.ibm.com/Cadet/MembersPages/ReportViewer.aspx">CADET</a>
    * <a href="https://auth.gainsightcloud.com/login#/">Gainsight</a>
* **Meet CS Account Specialists and Account Teams for all zSW assigned accounts**
    * Introduce yourself to your Account Specialist
    * Ask CS Account Specialist to introduce you to the Account Team
    * Get any insights, advice, etc. for your zSW accounts
* **Build the Plan**
    * Use these three critical criteria to build a meaningful plan with your customer and account teams:
        * Identify pain points with customers
        * Prioritize offerings based on customer pain points, ease of deployment (H,M,L) and revenue impact
        * Identify what customer is deploying now (quick wins)
* **Complete CSM Enablement (CADET & Gainsight Enablement included)**
    * ‘New to CS’ CSMs complete <a href="https://w3.ibm.com/w3publisher/customersuccess/being-a-csm/welcome-to-the-customer-success-team/csm-new-hire-onboarding">Onboarding</a>
        * Onboarding <a href="https://yourlearning.ibm.com/activity/PLAN-67610AFBAA6C">Technical Specialist plan</a>
    * ‘New to zSW’ CSMs complete YL <a href="https://yourlearning.ibm.com/activity/PLAN-AE67C1767A51">Brand Architect plan</a>
    * OPTIONAL: Complete <a href="https://yourlearning.ibm.com/activity/PLAN-B693F83B676A">Account Specialist plan</a> (should be viewed as complementary to the core YL Brand Architect plan for zSW CSMs)
    * KEY FOCUS AREA:  <a href="https://yourlearning.ibm.com/activity/PLAN-919BD88EA45F/track-progress?planIdFromParentTab=PLAN-2D7523B92E8A&sectionIdFromParentTab=SECTION-C&planIdForChildTab=PLAN-919BD88EA45F">YL plan</a> with key steps to enter growth plans into GS, create sizing (Price*Quantity) using Cadet (P) and Seismic Sales Configurator (Q):
* **Research Diamond Accounts:** Review Diamond ELA line items for CSM assigned accounts in Cadet
    * Identify account’s customer reporting timing (e.g. quarterly, 1H of year) for catalog & review pipeline of existing use cases/products and identify new use cases
* **Grow the Pipeline**
    * Perform Pipeline cleanup of records against CSM assigned Diamond ELA accounts.
    * Identify Growth Plans for CSM assigned Diamond ELA accounts in Gainsight to grow the pipeline.
        * Enter Growth Plan in Gainsight against growth offering (R360)
        * Perform sizing (P*Q) using Cadet and Seismic Sales Configurator
        * Update sizing in Gainsight
        * Monitor CADET to ensure pipeline reflects cleanup activities and growth plans
